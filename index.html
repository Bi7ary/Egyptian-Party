ُVirtual Fashion Festival in Siwa Oasis

Intro

"It is a wonderful and distinctive experience by creating a virtual environment that mimics one of the tourist shrines in the Siwa oasis and entering by default and taking a seat to see a special fashion show inspired by the Sioux heritage. We wish to enjoy this experience to learn more about fashion heritage and tourist places within Egypt. And this experience allows tourists from all over the world to explore the oasis of Siwa and its heritage without the need for actual travel as well as saving costs as the virtual festival reduces travel and accommodation costs, This makes it an economic choice for participants, encouraging them to visit it in the future."

Line One

"In the context of organizing a virtual fashion show inspired by the oasis of Siwa with the aim of attracting tourists, combining conceptual art methods with conceptual fashion shows to create a unique experience that combines heritage and innovation, where the idea and concept come first and foremost, and that conceptual costumes are not to wear for ordinary life, but include a message or idea and offer it in a special pleasant aesthetic style The conceptual presentation is about presenting fashion with an integrated experience "

Line Two

" You can see a tourist promotion of costumes inspired by the environmental makeup of the Siouh board, highlighting the tourist makeup of the board and making it not only a visit to geographical locations, but a comprehensive experience that reflects the identity and spirit of the place "

Line Three

"You can also see a fashion show of clothing presented as tourist memorabilia within the virtual environment of costumes that are distinct models of clothing and their supplements that bears the Egyptian heritage in a contemporary style to meet your needs and to be an ideal choice for those looking for comfortable and distinctive clothing"

Line Four

"There is also a fashion show inspired by the Sioux heritage that can be performed to attend the festival that relied on combining modern and future elegance with heritage details that characterize the Sioux culture to suit the atmosphere of virtual events, and the goal is not only to display clothes"
